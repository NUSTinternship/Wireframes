Stages of Progress
Proposal Stage

A student develops a proposal guided by their supervisor, once ready for evaluation the student gets approval from the supervisor to upload the approved proposal on the system. PhD students also upload a Similarity Index Report.

Thesis Stage

After the student successfully registered for the semester, then the relationship between the supervisor and the student is created in progress reports. Progress reports will also be uploaded by the student at this stage regularly.

Examination Stage

At this final stage, the student completes an intention to submit form, and uploads it. Once the form has been accepted, the student then uploads the approved thesis abstract for examiner appointment.
